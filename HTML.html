About Us

Fogg is a perfume and deodorant brand known for its long-lasting, gas-free formulations. They offer a variety of fragrances for both men and women, including options like Eau de Parfum, Extrait de Parfum, and perfume, catering to various preferences and occasions. Fogg's marketing often highlights its ability to leave a lasting impression with its captivating scents. Key aspects of Fogg perfumes: Long-lasting fragrance: Fogg is known for its extended fragrance retention, often lasting for several hours. Gas-free formula: A key selling point of Fogg is its lack of gas, which contributes to its long-lasting nature. Variety of scents: The brand offers a wide range of fragrances for both men and women, catering to different tastes and preferences. Dermatologically tested: Fogg's products are dermatologically tested to ensure safety and suitability for various skin types. Target audience: Fogg's marketing often targets young urbanites, with its edgy and rebellious brand identity. Effective Marketing: Fogg is known for its impactful marketing campaigns, including taglines like "Bina gas wala spray" and "Phir Khatam" (doesn't get blown away), which helped them gain popularity, says StartupTalky. Cruelty-free and safe: Fogg is considered cruelty-free and safe for use, according to Zoobop.
